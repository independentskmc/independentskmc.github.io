About Us

What are we!? The ideology of Independents


Making an idea a reality is the focal line of difference between Impossible and I'm Possible. We, at Independents KMC, have a vision of a shared table where students, no matter their differences in beliefs, share a common goal and embrace one another as family.


Student life is a mere handful of years where we dare to dream big. So why hold back? If there's a smouldering ember in your heart, let it burn bright.


Let's Unite to Ignite.
